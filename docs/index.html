University ofTEST York Baking Society

Welcome to my site!

We are a society with the goal of bringing people together to produce a variety of baked goods whether finely decorated masterpieces or soggy-bottomed splodges. All ingredients and the majority of baking apparatus is provided in our sessions. We usually aim to have at least 9 different themed sessions a term and a Baking Competition!
